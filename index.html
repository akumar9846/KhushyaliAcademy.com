COURSES


SSC


Staff Selection Commission (SSC) is an organisation under the Government of India to recruit staff for various posts
in the various ministries and departments of the government of India and in subordinate offices and agencies.





UPSC

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC; ISO: Saṁgha Loka Sevā Āyoga) is a constitutional body tasked with recruiting officers for All India Services and the Central Civil Services
(Group A and B) through various standardized examinations.


COURSES

BPSC


The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) is a government body under the state of Bihar, India, established by the Constitution of India, to recruit candidates for various state government jobs in Bihar through competitive examinations.
